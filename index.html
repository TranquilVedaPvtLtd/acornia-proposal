Platform Overview

What This Platform Does

This is a purpose-built business operating system for mutual fund distributors. It manages your complete investor lifecycle — from the first prospect meeting to ongoing portfolio reviews — with intelligent automation that tells your team what to do next, enforces compliance at every step, and gives the business owner live visibility into every metric that matters. Below is the complete scope of what is being built.

Complete Module Breakdown

17 Modules. Every Feature Listed.

Each module below is a fully functional part of the platform. This is not a feature wishlist — every item listed here will be delivered, tested, and operational at go-live.

Module 01

Investor Management

The central record of every investor family Acorn Wealth serves. Every detail — from PAN to bank accounts to investment history — accessible in seconds by any authorised team member.

  • Complete investor database with PAN-based unique identification
  • Investor 360 profile — 6-tab deep view (Profile, Tasks, Timeline, Folios, Reviews, Documents)
  • Family linking — group investors by family, show combined AUM
  • Tier classification — Platinum, Gold, Silver, Bronze — auto-calculated by AUM
  • Bank account details, nominee information, tax status tracking
  • Document storage — KYC forms, mandates, signatures, uploaded per investor
  • Advanced search — find any investor by name, PAN, phone, or email in under 2 seconds
  • Bulk import from CSV with duplicate detection and validation
Module 02

Transaction and Task Management

The operational core. Every mutual fund transaction — from SIP registration to redemption — is tracked as a task with a defined lifecycle, checklist, and owner.

  • 52+ pre-configured transaction types covering all MFD operations
  • Core: Purchase, SIP, SWP, STP, Switch, Redemption, NFO
  • Additional: KYC updates, mandate registration, folio consolidation, bank changes, nominee updates
  • 8-stage enforced workflow: Created, Assigned to Ops, In Progress, OTP Pending, Pending Verification, Completed, Failed, Cancelled
  • Auto-generated checklists per transaction type — different steps for SIP vs Switch vs Redemption
  • Priority management (High / Medium / Low) with SLA tracking
  • Platform-specific routing — tasks auto-tagged for NSE, BSE, or MFU processing
  • Sub-task support for complex multi-step transactions
  • Auto-generated unique transaction numbers for tracking
  • Bulk task creation and status updates
Module 03

Smart RM Action Queue

Every RM logs in to a prioritised list of actions. The system decides what needs attention — the RM focuses on execution, not on remembering deadlines.

  • Proactive daily task queue — the system tells each RM who to call and why
  • SIP lapse alerts — flagged 30, 15, and 7 days before mandate expiry
  • KYC renewal warnings — queued 60 days before expiry
  • Mandate expiry detection — daily automated checks at 7 AM
  • Dormant client surfacing — investors with no contact in 60+ days highlighted
  • Birthday reminders — automated daily at 8 AM with auto-created Birthday Wishes task
  • Overdue task escalation — every 4 hours, stuck tasks are flagged to the owner
  • Portfolio review reminders — weekly alerts for upcoming and overdue reviews
Module 04

Business Intelligence and Dashboards

Live dashboards that give the business owner complete visibility — without asking anyone to pull a report or open a spreadsheet.

  • Sales Dashboard — 10 live KPI cards: Total Investors, AUM, Calls Today, Reviews Due, Open Tasks, Completed Today, OTP Pending, SLA Breached, Avg TAT, Fresh Business
  • Fresh Business tracker — lumpsum + new SIPs this month vs target
  • Ongoing SIP Book — total monthly recurring SIP value
  • Analytics Dashboard — 6 intelligence tabs:
  •   Overview: firm-wide metrics at a glance
  •   Task Pipeline: transaction volume by type and status
  •   NTA/ETA: New-to-Acorn vs Existing-to-Acorn business split
  •   Compliance: KYC status, SLA adherence, document gaps
  •   Targets: monthly/quarterly targets vs achievement by RM
  •   Escalation: overdue tasks, SLA breaches, high-priority items
  • RM performance tracking — tasks completed, AUM managed, client acquisition vs attrition
Module 05

Prospect Pipeline

A structured pipeline for converting leads into investors. Every prospective client is tracked from first contact through conversion.

  • 7-stage sales funnel: New Lead, Contacted, Meeting Scheduled, Proposal Sent, Negotiation, Won, Lost
  • Source tracking — Referral, Digital, Event, Cold Call, Walk-In
  • Estimated AUM tracking per prospect
  • One-click conversion to investor — no data re-entry on onboarding
  • Activity history per prospect — every interaction logged
  • Kanban view of pipeline stages with drag-and-drop
  • Conversion rate tracking and source attribution analytics
Module 06

Portfolio Reviews

Systematic portfolio review scheduling ensures every investor family gets the periodic attention they deserve.

  • Scheduled review system with status tracking (Scheduled, In Progress, Completed, Overdue)
  • Automatic next-review scheduling — 6-month cycles created on completion
  • Overdue review detection and escalation to business owner
  • Review agenda and meeting notes capture
  • Action item tracking from each review session
  • Review history per investor — permanent advisory record
  • Calendar integration — reviews visible alongside tasks and activities
Module 07

Communication and Activity Log

Every client interaction — call, meeting, email, note — is logged, timestamped, and linked to the investor's permanent record.

  • 6 activity types: Call, Meeting, Email, Note, Follow-up, Review
  • Complete interaction history per investor
  • Duration and outcome tracking for calls and meetings
  • Follow-up automation — system creates follow-up tasks from logged activities
  • Auto-activity creation on task status changes
  • Color-coded timeline view with filtering by type and date
  • Activity export for compliance and reporting
Module 08

Calendar

A unified calendar that shows everything happening across the firm — tasks, meetings, reviews, and birthdays — in one view.

  • Monthly calendar grid with navigation
  • 4 event sources: Task due dates, Activities, Portfolio Reviews, Investor Birthdays
  • Color-coded event indicators per type
  • Day detail panel — click any date to see full event list
  • Quick event creation from calendar view
  • Holiday calendar configuration for SLA calculations
Module 09

Kanban Board

A visual drag-and-drop board for the Operations team to manage transaction flow across all 8 status stages.

  • 8 status columns: Created through Completed, Failed, Cancelled
  • Drag-and-drop to move tasks between stages
  • Card details: transaction type, investor name, amount, platform, priority, date
  • Board view and List view toggle
  • Filter by transaction type, priority, assigned RM
  • Visual count per column for workload assessment
Module 10

AI Meeting Processor

Upload a recording of any client meeting. The system transcribes it in full, extracts action items, and creates tasks automatically — reducing post-meeting admin from 60 minutes to under 5.

  • Audio recording upload — meetings, calls, voice notes
  • Full automatic transcription of the recording
  • AI extracts action items, investment preferences, and follow-up tasks
  • One-click task creation from extracted items — linked to investor record
  • Client preference capture (risk tolerance changes, retirement plans, etc.)
  • Meeting history per investor — searchable archive
  • Text-based meeting input for quick notes processing
Module 11

Automated Report Downloading and Data Sync

The platform automatically logs into investor and AMC portals, downloads reports, parses the data, and syncs it into the system — eliminating hours of manual report downloading and spreadsheet work every week.

  • Automated login to CAMS, Karvy/KFintech, and AMC portals
  • Scheduled report downloading — daily, weekly, or on-demand
  • Automatic data parsing and import into the platform
  • Portfolio valuation sync — current holdings, NAV, and value updated automatically
  • Transaction reconciliation — match platform records against downloaded statements
  • Mutual fund NAV price updates — daily automated sync from AMFI
  • SIP status sync — active, paused, cancelled SIPs detected from downloaded data
  • Capital gains data extraction for tax reporting
  • Error detection and discrepancy alerts when downloaded data doesn't match platform records
  • Complete history of all downloaded reports — searchable and exportable
Module 12

Compliance and Audit Trail

A complete, tamper-proof record of every action taken in the system — essential for SEBI inspections and internal reviews.

  • Complete change tracking — who changed what, when, with before/after values
  • Filter by entity (Investor, Task, Prospect), action (Create, Update, Delete), user, and date
  • SEBI audit-ready reports — exportable compliance records
  • KYC compliance monitoring — status distribution across all investors
  • SLA breach tracking — which tasks exceeded turnaround time
  • User activity audit — complete history of each team member's actions
Module 13

Data Import and Migration

All existing data — investors, transactions, activities, prospects — migrated into the new platform with validation and zero data loss.

  • CSV bulk import with column mapping interface
  • Automatic PAN-based duplicate detection
  • Validation reports showing errors per row before commit
  • Import history with status tracking
  • Support for Investor, Task, Prospect, and Activity imports
  • Complete migration from existing system — all historical data ported
  • Error log download for failed imports
Module 14

Notification System

Automated, event-driven alerts ensure nothing is missed by any team member.

  • In-app real-time notification bell with unread count
  • Email notifications for task assignments, completions, and escalations
  • SIP mandate expiry reminders — 30/15/7 days ahead
  • KYC renewal alerts — 60 days ahead
  • Birthday notification — daily at 8 AM
  • Escalation alerts — overdue tasks flagged every 4 hours
  • Review due reminders — weekly for upcoming reviews
  • Notification history — read/unread management
Module 15

Settings and Administration

Full control over users, roles, templates, targets, holidays, and system configuration — managed by the business owner without technical support.

  • User management — add, deactivate, assign roles for up to 22 users
  • 4 roles: Super Admin, Admin, Relationship Manager, Operations
  • Transaction checklist template management — customise steps per transaction type
  • Holiday calendar configuration — affects SLA calculations automatically
  • Monthly/quarterly target setting per RM and per team
  • Integration credentials management
  • Organisation profile and branding
  • Data export and backup controls
Module 16

Order Placement Integration

Direct connection to the NSE NMF II platform for placing mutual fund orders and tracking their execution status in real time.

  • Submit mutual fund orders directly from the platform
  • Real-time order status tracking — Pending, Executed, Rejected, Cancelled
  • Order-to-task linking — every order tied to its parent transaction
  • Automated execution confirmations
  • Status sync every 10 minutes — no manual checking needed
  • Secure encrypted communication with the exchange
Module 17

KYC Verification Integration

Real-time verification of investor KYC status against the central registry — no manual lookup, no separate portal login.

  • Real-time PAN verification against CVL KRA (Central KYC Registry)
  • Auto-triggered on investor creation and transaction submission
  • KYC status auto-determination: Verified, Pending, On Hold, Rejected, Not Verified
  • Batch KYC validation — check status for all investors at once
  • KYC renewal tracking — flags investors approaching KYC expiry
  • Blocks transaction submission if KYC is not verified — compliance enforced by the system
